Peachy Ventures is a UK software consultancy and product studio specialising in C++, embedded systems, signal processing, GPU/HPC, real-time systems, and audio/control software.

We help technical teams solve difficult software problems when the work is too specialised, too urgent, or too awkward.

What we do

Consultancy

Specialist systems software consultancy

We support technical teams facing hard software challenges, where the work is too specialised, time-sensitive, or awkward to hire for.

We are best suited to C++, embedded/Linux, GPU and high performance, real-time systems, audio/control software, signal processing, navigation and geometry, and complex integration or debugging work.

The focus is senior IC delivery: architecture where useful, but mainly implementation, debugging, optimisation, integration, and getting the work shipped.

Products

Independent tools for technical teams

We also build and operate our own products. Our flagship product, Sonoros, is live audio, routing, RF monitoring, and control software for theatre, live audio, and broadcast.

Sonoros is already deployed in a number of locations, from small in-house shows to major West End and touring musicals.

When to bring us in

Useful when the problem is technical, specific, and blocking delivery.

  • A C++/embedded/performance problem is blocking delivery.
  • A system needs debugging, integration, optimisation, or production hardening.
  • Your team needs senior implementation capacity without adding management overhead.
  • You are working close to hardware, devices, protocols, audio, RF, routing, or real-time constraints.
  • You need someone who can get into an existing codebase quickly and deliver.
