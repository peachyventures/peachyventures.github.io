Peachy Ventures is a pandemic baby, born in 2020 as a vehicle to create the things we think should exist in the audio space, but don't. It's headed up by a couple of nerds that have worked together on audio projects since primary school (really).

We want to create intuitive, robust products for theatre & events with a focus on audio. Our flagship product, Sonoros, is already deployed in a number of locations, from small in-house shows to major west end & touring musicals. We're very proud of it.
