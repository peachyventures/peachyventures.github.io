Sonoros v1.8

We've been working hard to bring you exciting new features & squash some pesky bugs in this release.

New in v1.8

- Added line check feature on macOS and iOS.
- Added ability to zoom the monitoring grid while editing layout on macOS.
- Edit mode now includes group edit controls, to lock these to editing while in a show.
- Improvements to the add devices view, including sorting of devices by manufacturer.
- You can now batch delete/rearrange players and devices.
- You can now view mics within their groups in filters, with an option to disable.
- Fixed a bug that could cause the window to resize itself in manage devices view.
- Fixed a bug that could cause Sonoros to crash when deleting a device.
- Fixed a bug that could cause battery times over 10 hours to clip on iOS.
- Fixed a bug that could lead to tickets not un-dimming on iOS when coming back online or restoring from a warning.
- Fixed a bug which means players assigned to a deleted device can’t be unassigned.
- Fixed a bug in iOS that could cause the active listen meter not to reset peak holding.
- Other performance improvements and bug fixes.


If you spot a bug or have an idea for a feature get in touch at support@peachy.ventures.
