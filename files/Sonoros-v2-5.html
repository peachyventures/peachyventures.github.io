Sonoros v2.5

Sonoros 2 is here! Packed with exciting new features, improvements & fixes.

New in v2.5

- Mirror Audio from clients to audio output on your server computer for IEM monitoring.
- Support for macOS & iOS 26.
- Improvements to iOS and macOS GUI.
- Other fixes and improvements.

New in v2

- Control routing of your wireless devices via DirectOut hardware right in Sonoros.
- Sonoros Virtual PatchBay for routing control and setup on macOS.
- New Channel Overview in Inspector with combined info & history chart, plus an overview of active warnings.
- Added support for Wisycom MRK980 and MCR54 with MRK16 receivers.
 - Added support for Sennheiser Spectera units. - The hidden state of the sidebar now persists between launches.
- Added keyboard shortcut for acknowledging warnings (Cmd + Shift + Space).
- Fixed an issue that could lead to toolbar buttons not being correct on macOS clients.
- Other fixes and improvements.
